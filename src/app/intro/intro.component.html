Welcome to the NEO Fintech Lab interview test. On the next page, you will see a basic currency converter for buying British Pounds (£GBP) and paying in Euros (€EUR).

A user can enter the amount in £GBP that they want to buy and the cost in €EUR will be displayed on screen. Currently, there is no way to enter the amount in €EUR that they want to sell and see the amount in £GBP that they will receive. Your task is to add this functionality.

User story

As a user, I want to select the amount to buy (£GBP) or the amount to sell (€EUR) and be shown the corresponding amount based on the current exchange rate, so that I can trade whether I need to buy a specific amount of £GBP or have a specific amount of €EUR to sell.

Acceptance criteria

  • I can enter either the sell amount or the buy amount, and the other field updates as the exchange rate changes
  • At all times, I can tell which field is locked (the one I typed in) and which field is reactive (the one that reacts to the exchange rate)
  • Entering an amount into either field will lock that field, making the other field reactive

Click 'trade' to move to the next page and see the trade form you'll be working on.
